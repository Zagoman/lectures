What is TypeScript and is it relevant?

In short, TypeScript is a superset of JavaScript, that allows you to define blueprints for the implementation of your application.

These blueprints give your IDE the chance of understanding what you are coding, and giving you great error checking while coding.

A few benefits of using TypeScript

  1. You get to catch erros a lot before they happen
  2. You rely less on console.log
  3. Your IDE becomes a bigger help by giving you great auto completion
  4. It is easier to work in groups when good principles of type safety are applied
  5. It is an "easy" language to learn, since any JavaScript is valid TypeScript
